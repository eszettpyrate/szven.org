21st of July, 2026
It was hot but not too much and I lay on my back on a warm rock and looked at the sky.
I was to myself and at peace, for the first time in weeks.
This is how the world should feel, and I wanted to capture that moment, and how beautifully the sunlight hit the leafs above me.

When I looked at the image I had taken later on, I was delighted by how incompetent I was. I'm really no photographer, but I don't mind it. I still thought the image turned out well, and it shows the hand of its creator.

However flawed it may be.
